Professional Summary

PhD student in Electrical Engineering at Penn State working on neuroscience-inspired learning algorithms and their robustness in neuromorphic settings. Research spans biologically-plausible local learning rules (Equilibrium Propagation), astrocyte-inspired neural computation, fault-tolerant training, and spiking neural networks.
